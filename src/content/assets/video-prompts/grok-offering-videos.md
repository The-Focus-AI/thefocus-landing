# Grok Video Prompts — Offering Pages

Copy-paste each prompt directly into Grok. All style rules are baked into every prompt.

## 1. Monthly Close

**Page:** `/offerings/monthly-close`

**Prompt:**

> 10-second video, 16:9 aspect ratio. Slow dolly right across a wooden office desk. The left side of the desk is cluttered with printed spreadsheets, a calculator, sticky notes, and a red pen marking errors. As the camera moves right, the clutter fades away and we arrive at a clean open laptop showing a dark interface with a green checkmark and the text "TheFocus.AI" on screen. Warm golden hour window light. Shallow depth of field. No text overlays except what is on the laptop screen. No voiceover.
